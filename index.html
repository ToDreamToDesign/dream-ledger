About

About DREAM Ledger

What This Is
A Personal Financial & Decision Archaeology System.
Not a budgeting app.
Not a portfolio tracker.
A system designed to preserve facts, decisions, reflections and consequences — so that the connection between choices and outcomes is never lost.
Why It Exists
This project was not born from a desire to get rich.

It began with watching a grandfather decline after a fall — being a university student with no income, no savings, and no ability to help.

Then a grandmother. Then military service. Then debt. Then responsibility.

Each experience left the same question unanswered until it couldn't be ignored anymore:

"If the people I care about need help — do I have the ability to provide it?"

DREAM Ledger is the answer being built.
Core Principles
Reality First
Refuse rounded numbers. Refuse comfortable approximations. Only real data can produce real understanding.
Event > Theory
What actually happened matters more than what should have happened. Record events before constructing explanations.
Anti Result Bias
A good outcome does not validate a bad process. A bad outcome does not invalidate a good one. Judge decisions by the quality of reasoning at the time they were made.
Responsibility Through Experience
Growth does not come from advice. It comes from experience. If one lesson is not enough, reality delivers another. Responsibility is not chosen — it is built through moments of powerlessness.
Core Belief
I do not pursue wealth for wealth itself.
I pursue capability.

Because I have experienced moments when the people I care about needed help — and I had nothing to offer.

The purpose of financial planning is not accumulation.
The purpose is preparedness, responsibility,
and the ability to help when it matters most.

Philosophy

理念

A Personal Financial & Decision Archaeology System

Why This Exists
有些事情只有親身經歷過，
才會明白它的重要。

當我看著家人面對疾病、
長期照護與生命的終點時，

我開始意識到：

很多時候，
真正令人遺憾的不是失去。

而是在重要的人需要幫助時，
自己沒有能力伸出手。

DREAM Ledger 誕生於這個問題。

它不是為了記錄金錢。

而是為了記錄那些塑造人生的選擇。
What This Is
財務紀錄告訴你，你的錢去了哪裡。

DREAM Ledger 幫助你理解，
為什麼你的決策塑造了你的人生。

不是記帳工具。
不是投資追蹤器。

決策考古系統。
Foundational Belief
人生的成長取決於所經歷的。

如果一次無法清醒，
現實必將再次給予提醒。

我被迫學會成長，
並決定背負自己應承擔的責任。
