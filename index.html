About

About DREAM Ledger

What This Is
A Personal Financial & Decision Archaeology System.
Not a budgeting app.
Not a portfolio tracker.
A system designed to preserve facts, decisions, reflections and consequences — so that the connection between choices and outcomes is never lost.
Core Principles
Reality First
Refuse rounded numbers. Refuse comfortable approximations. Only real data can produce real understanding.
Event > Theory
What actually happened matters more than what should have happened. Record events before constructing explanations.
Anti Result Bias
A good outcome does not validate a bad process. A bad outcome does not invalidate a good one. Judge decisions by the quality of reasoning at the time they were made.
Responsibility Through Experience
Growth does not come from advice. It comes from experience. If one lesson is not enough, reality delivers another. Responsibility is not chosen — it is built through moments of powerlessness.

Philosophy
DREAM Ledger 誕生於這個問題。
展開全文 ∨

我為何開始規劃人生。

當我看見重要的人進了醫院，

那份同意書所帶來的現實與殘酷，
讓我第一次真正思考：

如果今天有一個能降低風險的選項，
有一份能提供保障的準備，

我有能力替自己，
或替家人做出選擇嗎？

第一次的我沒有。
第二次的我有了，但還不夠。
那未來的第三次呢？
現實、責任、成長。
關於 DREAM Ledger 的起點。
Why This Exists
有些事情只有親身經歷過，
才會明白它的重要。
展開全文 ∨
當我看著家人面對疾病、
長期照護與生命的終點時，

我開始意識到：

很多時候，
真正令人遺憾的不是失去。

而是在重要的人需要幫助時，
自己沒有能力伸出手。

DREAM Ledger 不是為了記錄金錢。
而是為了記錄那些塑造人生的選擇。
