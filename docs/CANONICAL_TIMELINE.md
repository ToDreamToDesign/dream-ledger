# DREAM Ledger Canonical Timeline v1.0

## Project Identity

DREAM Ledger is not a budgeting application.

It is a Personal Financial & Decision Archaeology System.

Purpose:

* Record reality.
* Preserve decisions.
* Understand consequences.
* Learn from reflection.

Core Principles:

* Reality First
* Event > Theory
* Anti Result Bias

---

## Founder Background

The financial philosophy behind DREAM Ledger was not born from investing.

It originated from experiences of family illness, long-term care, responsibility, and the feeling of being unable to help when loved ones needed support.

The project exists because financial records alone cannot explain why a person became who they are.

# EVT-000

## 2020 — Grandfather Fall and Long-Term Bedridden Care

### What Happened

During university, grandfather suffered a serious fall.

Although he survived the initial medical treatment, his physical condition never fully recovered.

He entered long-term bedridden care.

### Personal Situation

At the time:

* University student
* No stable income
* No meaningful savings

Unable to provide financial or practical support.

### Outcome

Condition continued to decline.

Passed away in 2021.

### Long-Term Impact

First direct exposure to:

* Aging
* Long-term care
* Family caregiving pressure
* Medical reality

A seed was planted:

"If similar situations happen again in the future, I want to have the ability to help."

# Foundational Reflection

Growth does not come from advice.

Growth comes from experience.

Reality teaches lessons repeatedly until they are understood.

If a person does not awaken after one lesson,

reality eventually delivers another.

Grandfather's decline.

Grandmother's hospitalization.

Student loans.

Military service.

Debt.

Responsibility.

Career transitions.

All contributed to the same realization:

Responsibility is not suddenly acquired.

It is born from moments of powerlessness.

Moments when you wish you could help but cannot.

Those moments create the desire to become capable.

---

# Core Life Belief

I do not pursue wealth for wealth itself.

I pursue capability.

Because I have experienced moments when important people needed help and I had no ability to provide it.

I do not want to face that situation again.

The purpose of financial planning is not accumulation.

The purpose is preparedness, responsibility, and the ability to help when it matters most.
