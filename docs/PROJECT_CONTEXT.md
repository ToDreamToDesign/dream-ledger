## What is DREAM Ledger?

DREAM Ledger is a **Personal Financial & Decision Archaeology System**.

It is not a budgeting app. It is not a net-worth tracker.

It is a record of why a person became who they are — through the lens of money, decisions, and time.

---

## Why Does It Exist?

The project was born from personal experience:

- 2020: Grandfather's fall, long-term bedridden care, and eventual passing in 2021.
- During that time: no stable income, no savings, no ability to help.
- The seed: "If this happens again, I want to have the ability to help."

Financial records alone cannot explain this origin. DREAM Ledger preserves the full context.

See [CANONICAL_TIMELINE.md](CANONICAL_TIMELINE.md) for the complete narrative.
